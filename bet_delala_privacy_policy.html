1. Introduction

Welcome to Bet Delala (mobile) ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. Please also read our Terms of Service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our App to you.

A. Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Registration: When you create an account, we collect personal information, which includes your Full Name, Phone Number, Email Address, and Password. You may also optionally provide your City and Street.
  • User-Generated Content: When you post a property listing, we collect all the information you provide, including property details (price, area, bedrooms, etc.), descriptions, property photos you upload, and any optional YouTube links.
  • Communications: If you contact us directly (e.g., for customer support), we may receive additional information about you.

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Location Data: When you use the location picker to post a property, we collect your precise geolocation (latitude and longitude). We may also request access to your device's current location to help you find your position on the map.
  • Usage and Device Data: We may automatically collect standard device information (e.g., device model, OS version, IP address) and usage data (e.g., features you use, ads you view).

C. Information from Third Parties

  • Advertising Partners: We use third-party advertising services like Google AdMob. These services may collect information (such as your device's advertising ID) to provide personalized advertisements. You can learn more about how Google handles your data here.
  • Mapping Services: We use Google Maps/Places API to provide mapping and address search functionality. Your use of these features is subject to the Google Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our App.
  • To create and manage your account and property listings.
  • To display your property listings to other users.
  • To send you administrative or support messages, including password resets.
  • To display relevant (personalized or non-personalized) advertisements.
  • To analyze and improve the App's functionality and user experience.
  • To prevent fraud and enforce our Terms of Service.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following situations:

  • Publicly: Any information you include in a property listing (photos, description, location) will be publicly visible to other users of the App.
  • With Third-Party Service Providers: We share data with services that help us run the App, such as hosting providers, image upload services, and analytics providers.
  • With Advertising Partners: As mentioned, we share data with advertising partners like Google AdMob to show ads in the App.
  • With Mapping Services: Information (like location data and search queries) is shared with Google to power the Maps and Places features.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
