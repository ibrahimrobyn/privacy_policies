Privacy Policy for Esoora Express

Effective Date: February 13, 2025

1. Introduction

At Ethio Clicks, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy applies to Esoora Express, a goods delivery application developed by Ethio Clicks. It explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you register or place an order, we may collect your name, email address (optional), phone number, and delivery address.
  • Payment Information: To process orders, we collect payment details necessary for the transaction. Payment information is handled securely by our payment service providers; we do not store full credit card details.
  • Location Data: To facilitate order tracking and efficient delivery, we may collect and process your location data while you use the app.
  • Order Information: We record details related to your orders, including order history, delivery instructions, and preferences.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Order Processing: To fulfill and track your orders efficiently.
  • Communication: To send you updates, confirmations, and support messages regarding your orders and account.
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and enhance app functionality and user experience.
  • Marketing: To inform you about promotions or updates, only where you have provided consent.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share your information only with trusted service providers (such as payment processors and delivery partners) who are required to protect your data. We may also disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights and the safety of our users.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored on servers managed by Ethio Clicks. We employ industry-standard security measures—including encryption and access controls—to safeguard your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to process orders, deliver services, and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting our support team.

7. Your Rights

Access and Update: You have the right to access and update your personal information within the app.

Data Deletion: You may request that we delete your data, and we will comply as soon as practically possible.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our mobile app does not use cookies. We may use similar tracking technologies solely for analytics and to improve your experience.

9. Legal Compliance

We comply with all applicable data protection regulations and laws. By using Esoora Express, you consent to our collection and use of your data as described in this Privacy Policy.
